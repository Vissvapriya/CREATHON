Government Policies & Incentives

Governments worldwide are introducing regulations to curb single-use plastic consumption. Adopting sustainable practices helps businesses comply with these policies.

  • India: Ban on single-use plastic items, including plastic bags, straws, and packaging
  • Global: Countries such as the EU and Canada are enacting similar plastic bans
  • Incentives: Tax benefits and subsidies for businesses adopting eco-friendly practices

Certifications & Standards

Eco-certifications enhance credibility with customers and help businesses stand out. Here are some valuable certifications:

  • ISO 14001: Environmental Management System certification
  • Plastic Neutral Certification: Reduces environmental impact by offsetting plastic waste
  • Fair Trade Certified: Recognizes businesses supporting sustainable practices

Obtaining these certifications can improve operational efficiency and appeal to a broader customer base.
